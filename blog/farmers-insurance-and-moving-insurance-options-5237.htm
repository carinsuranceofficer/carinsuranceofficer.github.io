Farmers Insurance and Moving Insurance Options

Home / Blog / Blog Details

Moving to a new home is one of life’s most exciting yet stressful experiences. Whether you’re relocating across town or across the country, the process involves significant financial and emotional investment. With global supply chain disruptions, rising inflation, and extreme weather events becoming more frequent, protecting your belongings during a move has never been more critical. Farmers Insurance offers a range of moving insurance options designed to safeguard your possessions—but how do you choose the right coverage?

Why Moving Insurance Matters in Today’s World

The Rising Cost of Household Goods

Inflation has driven up the cost of furniture, electronics, and other household items. Replacing damaged or lost belongings out of pocket can be financially devastating. Moving insurance ensures that you won’t bear the full burden if something goes wrong during transit.

Climate Change and Extreme Weather Risks

Hurricanes, wildfires, and floods are increasingly common, posing risks to belongings in transit. Farmers Insurance provides coverage options that account for unpredictable weather, giving you peace of mind no matter the forecast.

Supply Chain Delays and Shortages

Global supply chain issues mean that replacing lost or damaged items could take months. Moving insurance helps mitigate these delays by covering repair or replacement costs promptly.

Farmers Insurance Moving Coverage Options

Farmers offers several insurance solutions tailored to different moving scenarios. Here’s a breakdown of the most popular options:

1. Basic Released Value Protection

This is the most economical option, typically included by default with professional movers. However, it provides minimal coverage—usually just $0.60 per pound per item. If your $1,000 laptop is damaged, you might only receive $10 in compensation.

Best for: Those moving low-value items or willing to assume most of the risk.

2. Full Value Protection

For more comprehensive coverage, Full Value Protection ensures that Farmers will repair, replace, or reimburse you for the full value of damaged or lost items. This option is ideal for high-value belongings like antiques, artwork, or electronics.

Best for: Movers with expensive or irreplaceable items who want maximum financial security.

3. Third-Party Moving Insurance

If you’re using a moving company that doesn’t offer sufficient coverage, Farmers can supplement with third-party insurance. This is especially useful for long-distance or international moves where risks are higher.

Best for: Those using independent movers or relocating overseas.

4. Renter’s or Homeowner’s Insurance Extensions

Existing Farmers policies may already cover some moving-related damages. Check if your renter’s or homeowner’s insurance includes temporary moving protection.

Best for: Current Farmers customers looking for seamless coverage during relocation.

How to Choose the Right Moving Insurance

Assess the Value of Your Belongings

Take inventory of your items before the move. High-value possessions may require additional riders or specialized coverage.

Understand the Fine Print

Not all policies cover every type of damage. For example, some exclude mechanical failures (like a refrigerator breaking down) or damages caused by improper packing.

Compare Costs vs. Coverage

While basic protection is cheap, it may not be sufficient. Weigh the cost of premiums against potential out-of-pocket expenses in case of loss.

Final Tips for a Stress-Free Move

  • Pack strategically: Use high-quality boxes and padding for fragile items.
  • Document everything: Take photos of valuable items before packing.
  • Consider climate-controlled storage: If there’s a delay, extreme temperatures won’t ruin your belongings.

With Farmers Insurance, you can navigate the uncertainties of moving with confidence. Whether you’re facing economic instability, extreme weather, or logistical challenges, the right coverage ensures your new chapter starts on solid ground.
